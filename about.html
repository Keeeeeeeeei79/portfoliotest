About Me.

Who I Am

I'm Kei Endo, originally from Shizuoka City, Japan — a place known for its beautiful views of Mount Fuji. I grew up near the mountain and it shaped my appreciation for nature, discipline, and perseverance.

Before coming to New Zealand, I worked as a physiotherapist in Japan, helping patients recover and improve their quality of life. It was a rewarding career that taught me the importance of understanding people, communicating effectively, and staying committed to long-term goals.

Now based in Auckland, I'm working as a barista while studying at ICL. Both physiotherapy and barista work share a common thread — they're both about connecting with people and delivering quality through attention to detail.
